# Synthesis

**Automate. Survive. Cure.**

In a world overrun by the infected, humanity's last hope lies in the power of industry. **Synthesis** is a voxel-based survival game that blends high-stakes combat with complex factory automation. You aren't just fighting to live another day; you are engineering the salvation of the human race.

## The Mission

The infection spreads fast, but your machines run faster. Your goal is simple but daunting: synthesize the cure. To do this, you must harvest resources, build supply chains, and research lost technologies while holding back the relentless undead.
